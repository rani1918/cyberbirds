Our Story

Who We Are

Founded in 2023, Cyber Birds emerged from a shared vision to bridge the cybersecurity knowledge gap. We recognized that while digital threats were growing increasingly sophisticated, many individuals and small businesses lacked access to practical, understandable security guidance.

What began as a passion project among cybersecurity professionals has grown into a trusted resource for thousands seeking to navigate the digital world safely. Our team combines technical expertise with a talent for making complex concepts accessible.

Our Mission

Our Mission

At Cyber Birds, we believe cybersecurity awareness should be accessible to everyone, not just technical professionals. Our mission is to demystify digital security through clear, actionable guidance that empowers individuals and organizations to protect themselves in an increasingly complex online landscape.

We're committed to creating a safer digital ecosystem by educating users about threats, promoting responsible online behavior, and advocating for security best practices across all levels of society. Through our initiatives, we aim to reduce vulnerabilities and build digital resilience worldwide.

Rani Giri

Founder & CEO

Rani Giri is a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast and the founder of Cyber Birds, a student-led initiative aimed at spreading awareness about digital safety. Currently pursuing her degree in Computer Science, Rani has been exploring the world of cybersecurity and software development for the past few years through college projects, online communities, and self-driven learning.

Cyber Birds is part of one such initiative, developed as a college project to help students and non-technical users understand how to stay safe online. Rani was inspired to start this after seeing how everyday users—especially those without technical backgrounds—struggle with cyber threats.
